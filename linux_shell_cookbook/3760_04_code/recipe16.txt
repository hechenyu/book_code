To print 3 lines after the match line along with match line
===========================================================
$ grep -A 3 "Cameron Diaz" actress_rankings.txt
5 Cameron Diaz 
6 Annie Potts 
7 Liv Tyler

To print 3 lines before match line including match line
=======================================================
$ grep -B 3 "Cameron Diaz" actress_rankings.txt 
2 Natalie Portman 
3 Monica Bellucci 
4 Bonnie Hunt 
5 Cameron Diaz

===========================================================================
